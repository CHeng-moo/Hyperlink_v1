Biggest Wombat Ever?

This viral photo shows just how massive a wombat can appear — and yes, it’s real. According to the original Reddit thread, this wombat isn't abnormally large because of photoshop or trick photography. It's just a very big wombat.

Commenters pointed out that wombats can weigh between 20 to 35 kilograms (44–77 lbs), with some even reaching 40 kg. The one in this photo seems especially hefty, especially when held by a human — probably due to perspective and the animal's relaxed, plushy build.

One top-voted comment simply said: "This isn't a wombat. It's a wombeast." 🐾
