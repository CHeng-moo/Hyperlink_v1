Is My Snail Dead?

If you've found your snail unresponsive, still, and possibly stuck inside its shell — don't panic. You're not alone. One Reddit user asked this exact question and sparked a surprisingly wholesome thread.

Turns out, snails can sleep for a long time. Like, days. Some species may go into a kind of hibernation or estivation if the environment isn't right — too dry, too cold, or too warm.

Top-voted advice? Try gently misting the snail’s tank or placing the snail in a shallow dish of water. Sometimes that’s enough to wake them up. If their body is still soft and retracted, they may just be snoozing.

Signs that your snail is really gone? A strong smell, a dried-out body, or the trapdoor (operculum) missing or loose.
