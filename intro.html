Hey — since you clicked in, here’s a quick note.

This site is part of a personal project where I’m looking into how people read online — not just what they read, but how they move through it.
You know, the little things we barely notice:
where we click, when we drift, whether we ever make it back to where we started.

While you’re here, the site quietly records:

  • which links you click
  • the order of your path
  • how long you stay on each page

That’s it.
No names, no accounts, no tracking beyond this tiny space.
Just a soft trail of how you wandered — for me to look at later, as part of this project.

Thanks for being part of it, even if you didn’t mean to be.
(That’s kind of what makes it interesting.)
